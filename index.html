How Rentlyhood Works

How to Rent?

  • Download the Rentlyhood app and sign up
  • Search for the required item
  • Choose dates and submit the booking request
  • Make payment once lender approves booking request
Get Started

How to Lend?

  • Download the Rentlyhood app and sign up
  • Switch to Lender profile and click 'Create New Listing'
  • Enter details of item and upload photos
  • Submit for Approval
